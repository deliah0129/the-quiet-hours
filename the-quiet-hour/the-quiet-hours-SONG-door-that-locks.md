# “THE DOOR THAT LOCKS” — Jae’s song *(her counterpart to “Under”)*

*Her song to his. Built on her whole goal-motif — the desk, the small brass nameplate, the door no one can revoke — turned by the discovery that she left it open. The his/hers mirror is exact: his song is “a light in a window I wasn’t looking for” (her, three years ago); hers is “someone left the warm light on for me, and I stayed” (him, now). The light is each other. **Female vocal, spare indie alt-pop, withheld then cracking.***
