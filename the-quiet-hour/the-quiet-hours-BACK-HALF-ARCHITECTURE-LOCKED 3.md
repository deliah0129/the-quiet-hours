### The mechanism, airtight (the ⚙️ beat)

The Ch27 reveal makes Jae the recovered Daon blood heir. Overnight that makes her two things:

1. **Legally exposed / controllable** — a surfaced heir with no standing, no resources, contested legitimacy. The Daon corporate machine and the half-sister’s camp can subsume her, make decisions for her, freeze or steer her, drag her into a legitimacy fight she can’t fund. She has no autonomy.
1. **A press sensation** — “lost media-dynasty heir surfaces,” colliding with the idol exposure (Doha = Seungwoo) and the ex’s outing. She is trivially painted as impostor / opportunist; her story is owned by everyone but her.

A registered marriage to Do-yun does three jobs in one stroke:

- **Legal standing / autonomy** — married, tied to the Kang/SEORÉ house, she gains independent footing + a counterweight that can field lawyers. She can’t be quietly subsumed or steered by Daon. *Her own door, even here.*
- **Narrative control** — “she’s the wife” is a fixed, legitimate, un-spinnable status; it takes the story out of the press’s hands.
- **Kills his merger** — the same stroke pre-empts the Seo/Yuna merger. He has his *own* reason to say yes → it’s **mutual, not charity** → a can’t-receive woman can accept it.

### The two logic joints (must survive a skeptical reread)

- **Why is she vulnerable, not safe, as the heir?** No standing, no money, contested legitimacy; the half-sister’s side (canon: *eyeless-of-the-trait*) is the **legal/PR adversary** — contests her claim, paints her impostor, fights via lawyers and press, not goons.
- **Why doesn’t the searching grandmother just shield her?** Practically: the grandmother is the *searcher*, not the throne — the family’s fractured, she’s outgunned by an entrenched faction. Thematically (the better answer): accepting her protection = being *claimed*, absorbed, installed — the wound in kinder clothes (owned instead of erased). Jae won’t trade one cage for a nicer one. The marriage is the only protection she **negotiates and controls** (fixed-term, her terms).

### Guardrails (canon, do not move)

- **The heir reveal TESTS the love, never rewards it. “Love earned before, never the money.”** The shield can NEVER read as *his wealth saves her*. Protection = standing + narrative + the mutual merger-kill. Wealth stays incidental.
- **She asks** (the can’t-receive valve forced open; asking the betrayer = the hardest thing her character can do). **His yes is love; “it’s just strategy” is the lie she tells herself.**
- **The Ch25 injury is the EX’s doing** (press crush), separate from the heir machinery. Cutting the “disappear her” threat orphans nothing.
