# “RIGHT ON TIME” — KAIROS title track

*The group’s self-produced comeback title track — the record that makes them theirs and not the company’s (the autonomy bid that dies if Do-yun is retired). Classic K-pop title-track structure: rap verses (Reo, with Yejun), vocal lines (Do-yun main, Tae, Sun), big anthemic fan-chant chorus. Theme: basement-to-stage, fifteen years, chosen brotherhood, we built our own door — and we came on OUR time, not theirs. Title plays on KAIROS (Greek: the decisive/opportune moment). Style 1 + 2: anthemic comeback energy fused with edgier hip-hop self-production.*

**MEMBERS:** Tae (leader, steady) · Reo (rapper, blunt) · Sun (maknae, warmth) · Yejun (dry, precise) · Do-yun (main vocal / visual / center). Member tags below are intent for arrangement; Suno won’t assign them, but they guide who-sings-what if produced properly.
