# THE QUIET HOURS — CANON SYNC, 2026-06-13 · THE BANTER DIAL

**What this is:** a push-ready record of the voice decision made this session — *keep the banter, dial it up, lose nothing of the slow-burn underneath* — written into every control doc that a future drafting pass reads, so the register can't drift in either direction.

## PART A — THE DIAL (canonical statement)

**Register: first-person past, dual-POV — literary slow-burn with the banter dial UP (banter-hybrid).** Locked 2026-06-13.

**The substance does not change.** The dual-POV register split (Do-yun pours / Jae withholds — `register-split.md`), the Jae Guard, the restraint, the charged-beat discipline, and show-don’t-tell are the slow-burn engine. They keep getting exactly the treatment the chapter passes have been giving them.

**The banter is the surface, dialed up.** Comedy sparks in three places:
- **the dialogue** — fast, tag-light volleys, the verbal spar *is* the flirtation;
- **Do-yun’s extrovert surface** — charm is his trained idol skin (he fills silence, reads the room, processes outward);
- **the ensemble** — KAIROS, Pak, Halmeoni, the diner, the market: a comic chorus that heckles and outs feelings the leads won’t admit.

**What the banter may never do (the floor it can’t cross):**
1. **Displace the dry interiority or the restraint.** The feeling still gets turned over for the bill before it’s allowed to land; the lush still rations to charged beats. Banter is the surface; the slow-burn is the substance.
2. **Land *on* the charged beat.** Comedy *surrounds* the ache and goes **silent** for it — the poured water, the heartbeat under her palm, the song moving — lets it play clean, then resumes (“It’s a quieter nothing.”). Heaviness lands because it’s framed by light, never because the narrator quipped over it.
3. **Climb into Jae’s narration.** She is the dry introvert; her comedy is the deadpan **gap** — the silent verdict, the unsaid line (“imagine paying a man that much to stand still”) — never the volley. The verbal engine is Do-yun’s and the room’s. **A motormouth Jae breaks the book.**

**Dual anti-drift — both directions are errors:**
- ✗ **Do not** read this as the retired *“banter-forward commercial / Tessa-Bailey lane / dialogue-carries-everything”* conversion. That misread makes banter *replace* interiority and nearly wrecked the prose. Banter is surface, not substance.
- ✗ **Do not** austerify the banter back out to match a *“pure / austere literary slow-burn”* label. Keeping the spark is canon as of 2026-06-13. A pass that quiets the dialogue because a stale label says austere is **reverse-drift** — the exact failure this entry exists to prevent.

**Governing spec:** `register-split.md` (sentence-level) + this dial. `house-style-comedic.md` = **banter-toolkit reference only** (mine for comic mechanics; NOT the master register doc).
